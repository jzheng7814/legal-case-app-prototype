SOFTWARE LICENSING AGREEMENT

This Software Licensing Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 10, 2024 ("Effective Date"), between Sarah Johnson, a sole proprietor doing business as Johnson Tech Consulting ("Licensee"), and XYZ Corporation, a Delaware corporation ("Licensor").

RECITALS

WHEREAS, Licensor has developed proprietary software solutions for business operations management; and
WHEREAS, Licensee desires to license and use such software for her consulting business operations; and
WHEREAS, the parties wish to set forth the terms and conditions of such license;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

1. GRANT OF LICENSE
1.1 Subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement, Licensor hereby grants to Licensee a non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use the Software for Licensee's internal business operations.
1.2 This license includes access to all current software modules, documentation, and related materials.

2. LICENSE FEE
2.1 In consideration for the rights granted herein, Licensee shall pay Licensor a one-time license fee of Seventy-Five Thousand Dollars ($75,000) ("License Fee").
2.2 The License Fee was paid in full upon execution of this Agreement.

3. TERM
This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period of three (3) years, unless earlier terminated in accordance with the provisions hereof.

4. SUPPORT AND MAINTENANCE

4.1 Technical Support Standards
Licensor shall provide technical support services with the following performance standards:
(a) Response time for all support requests shall not exceed twenty-four (24) hours
(b) Resolution of critical issues within seventy-two (72) hours of initial contact
(c) Availability of support personnel during business hours (8 AM - 6 PM PST, Monday-Friday)

4.2 SOFTWARE UPDATES AND ENHANCEMENTS
Licensor agrees to provide:
(a) Regular software updates on a quarterly basis to ensure optimal performance
(b) Compatibility updates for new operating systems within thirty (30) days of OS release
(c) Security patches and bug fixes as needed
(d) Access to new features and enhancements developed during the license term

4.3 ON-SITE SUPPORT
Upon reasonable request, Licensor shall provide on-site technical assistance within forty-eight (48) hours. On-site support includes:
(a) Installation and configuration assistance
(b) Training for Licensee's personnel
(c) Troubleshooting of complex technical issues
(d) System optimization and performance tuning

5. PERFORMANCE STANDARDS
5.1 All software updates must maintain compatibility with current industry standards
5.2 System uptime shall be maintained at ninety-nine percent (99%) or higher
5.3 All modifications must preserve data integrity and system security
5.4 Documentation shall be provided for all updates and changes

6. BREACH AND REMEDIES
6.1 Material Breach: Any failure to meet the service levels specified in Section 4 shall constitute a material breach of this Agreement.
6.2 Notice and Cure: The non-breaching party shall provide written notice of breach, and the breaching party shall have thirty (30) days to cure.
6.3 Remedies: Upon uncured material breach, the non-breaching party may seek damages, specific performance, or terminate this Agreement.

7. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
EXCEPT FOR BREACHES OF SECTION 4 (SUPPORT AND MAINTENANCE), IN NO EVENT SHALL EITHER PARTY BE LIABLE FOR CONSEQUENTIAL, INCIDENTAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES.

8. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of California.
